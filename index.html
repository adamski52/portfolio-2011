Projects

It's a funny thing about the creative industry; employers want to see work samples and then hand you a nondisclosure agreement barring you from discussing your work. One must wonder if they expect you to honor their agreement when they ask you not to honor others'.

I do honor my agreements. My professional integrity has afforded me the good fortune to work on projects whose scale and impact necessitates secrecy as much as it does my technical expertise.

In a very real way, there are products and technologies whose existence may not have been possible without my contributions. I take great pride in that and want to continue working on the cutting edge of various industries, implementing technologies not yet seen outside of the lab.
